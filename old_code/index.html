Welcome!


Jess Weichler is a media arts educator in Wellington, New Zealand. Over the past five years she has worked with students ages five to eighteen, in both formal and informal classroom settings. Not content to teach just one age group, Jess is also experienced in training teachers in the fields of video editing, video game design, and stop-motion animation.


A self-motivated, life-long learner, Jess is constantly seeking out new mediums to enrich herself and others. In 2013 she participated in a week-long Maker Boot Camp at the Children's Museum of Pittsburgh (USA). During this week-long professional development workshop she worked along-side peers and experts to discover new ways to implement Making into classrooms.


In her spare time, Jess designs and maintains websites for small, independent businesses and volunteers for Mainly Music, a weekly music session for toddlers and their caregivers.


Up until this year, Jess has been teaching in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania (USA), but she is excited to bring her skills and unique expertise to Wellington, New Zealand in 2014.

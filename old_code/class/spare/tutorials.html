about

At Pittsburgh Center for the Arts we pride ourselves on teaching process over product. This course is no different. In Intro to Video Game Design we strive to encourage our students to think like programmers; instead of presenting them with a list of answers we have them find questions that need to be solved. We believe this helps students to continue to learn and grow in the ever changing world of technology long after their time with us has ended.


Whether student, parent, or educator, we encourage you to use our lesson plans and tutorials to continue the educational process.

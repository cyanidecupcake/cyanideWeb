conductive paintings.

Jess developed the idea of creating interactive paintings in 2014 after purchasing far too much conductive paint for a class. With the leftovers she created her first conductive painting which depicted 2D musical instruments that could be played by the viewer through touch.

Conductive paintings are far more than art, they also teach the basics of electricity in a tangible and fun way.

love letter to pittsburgh 2015

Made with electronics, code, and conductive paint this artwork is a love letter to Pittsburgh, where rivers converge and technology reigns.

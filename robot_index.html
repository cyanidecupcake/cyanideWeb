I hope you had a great day in class! Today we learned all about circuits and electricity. If you would like to make conductive dough creations at home, you can use the recipe below. Please make sure to ask a parent for help!

conductive dough

ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 3 Tbsp cream of tartar*
      9 Tbsp lemon juice may be subsituted
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • food coloring of your choice

  • 1/2 cup flour

  1. 1. Mix ingredients together in a medium sized pot, omitting 1/2 cup flour.
  2. 2. Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously.
  3. 3. The mixture will boil and begin to solidify. Keep stirring until it forms a ball
  4. 4. Once ball forms, place the ball on a lightly floured surface. Flatten out the dough a bit and let cool before handling.
  5. 5. Once the dough is cool, slowly knead the 1/2 cup of flour into the ball until you've reached the desired consistancy.
  6. 6. When not in use you can store the dough in an airtight container or plastic bag and place it in the fridge.

JESS WEICHLER is a media arts educator from Pittsburgh, PA. Since 2009 she has been working as a media arts educator, teaching young and old how to unleash their creativity using technology.

Through merging art and technology, Jess has found her own creative voice. In 2014 she began making conductive paintings that allow the viewer to interact with her art. These paintings are more than just art, they also teach scientific concepts!

Determined to never be bored, Jess loves exploring various mediums. In her spare time she designs websites for small businesses and enjoys photographing nature.

Much of her work is available via Creative Commons and Open Source licenses, which ever is applicable.

Projects

Hear Me 101 {Carnegie Mellon University, CREATE Lab} February 2012 – January 2014

Hear Me 101 is a year-long program based in public schools located in economically disadvantaged neighborhoods. Over the course of a year students create short documentaries from concept to final cut. The goal is to guide students to professionally express their thoughts and opinions on their communities and their education, allowing them to have their voice heard by education and community decision makers.

  • As classroom leader at McKeesport High School I was responsible for creating and enacting lesson plans and keeping an open line of communication between the regular classroom instructor and the rest of the Hear Me staff
  • Helped students achieve their vision for their documentary
  • Worked with all four of the Hear Me 101 schools, teaching students how to properly use their digital cameras and conducted editing workshops.
